# Course Schedule

This course is organized into **six days**. Each day is designed to take roughly **5–6 hours** of focused study: a mix of reading the concept lessons, watching/following along, and completing the hands-on lab. You can compress or stretch the schedule to fit your pace.

> The "Time" column is a suggestion, not a deadline. Spend extra time on the labs — that is where the learning sticks.

## Suggested Weekly Plan

If you are studying part-time, here is one way to spread the six days across two weeks:

- **Week 1:** Day 1 (Mon), Day 2 (Wed), Day 3 (Fri)
- **Week 2:** Day 4 (Mon), Day 5 (Wed), Day 6 (Fri/weekend)

Take notes, complete every lab, and revisit the [Interview Questions](./Resources/Interview-Questions.md) at the end.
